Bing for Commerce

[Main Portal]

Microsoft Bing for Commerce is an out-of-the-box solution for retailers to easily and securely enable intelligent search, personalization, and product recommendations, leveraging on Azure Cloud and Bing's AI infrastructure.

I was responsible for the intelligent autosuggestions on Bing for Commerce — building the data ingestion/ranking pipelines and the services to return the autosuggestions. I also worked closely with downstream clients to ensure their business requirements are met, such as ensuring data privacy compliancy, and addressing new requests.
