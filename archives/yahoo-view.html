Yahoo! View

[Landing Page]

Yahoo View! was a TV streaming service powered by Hulu and hosted thousands of TV shows and movies. At the time, it was one of the only ways to watch Hulu's TV shows and movies for free.

We were given a very tight deadline of 7 weeks to complete the project from start to finish. To ensure a successful launch, I worked throughout the entire software stack and provided support where-ever it is needed. I worked closely with designers to bring Yahoo! View from ideation to reality and built out key user flows, such as the landing page, search page, show page, and watch page. I also helped with key backend business logic, optimizing database queries, implementing AWS CloudSearch for improved performance, and integrating Tumblr content into Yahoo View for engagement.
